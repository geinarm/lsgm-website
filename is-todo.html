Here are some places that we love and hope you will too! Please ask us if you have questions!

Ísafjörður (near the venue)

Tjöruhúsið
One of the oldest houses in Ísafjörður that has been transformed into a restaurant. Best seafood buffet anywhere, ever.

Neðstikaupstaður
The oldest remaining house in town has been made into a museum that shows what life was like as a fisherman in Iceland in the 1700s.

Húsið

Reykjavík Area

Golden Circle
All the Icelandic postcard locations in one quick drive.

Blue Lagoon
The blue lagoon is a must for anyone traveling to Iceland. Its a giant, blue swimming pool located in the middle of a lava field just outside of Reykjavík.
Some people are uncomfortable with the casual nakedness on display in Icelandic swimming pools.

Pylsa
From the mightiest king to the loneliest peasant, every one enjoys an Icelandic hot dog.
