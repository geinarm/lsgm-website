Neðstikaupstaður
The oldes remaining house in town has been made into a museum that shows what life was like as a fisherman in Iceland in the 1700s.

Reykjavík Area

Blue Lagoon
The blue lagoon is a must for anyone traveling to Iceland. Its a giant, blue swimming pool located in the middle of a lava field just outside of Reykjavík.
Some people are uncomfortable with the casual nakedness on display in Icelandic swimming pools.
