The ceremony and reception will take place in the historic Springfield barn. The barn which overlooks Williamsport's Byron Memorial Park was built circa 1755 by Otho Holland Williams, the founder of Williamsport. While some renovations have been made over the years, the barn still possesses the majority of its original materials. Lauren and Guðjón first saw the barn while attending a town Christmas event with Lauren's family. They immediately fell in love with the historic look and feel.
