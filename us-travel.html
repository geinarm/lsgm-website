Airports: So many choices!

Dulles International Airport: 80 minutes from venue
BWI: 80 minutes from venue
Reagan National Airport: 80 minutes from venue
Harrisburg International Airport: 90 minutes from venue

Pro Tip: When booking flights use the the airport code WAS to search Dulles, Reagan and BWI simultaneously. You will have to to search for Harrisburg flights separately.

Flying from Iceland

We would recommend booking your tickets through Iceland Air or Wow Air. Iceland Air is amazing and is never a bad choice. If you book early (like yesterday) you can get a good price and they give you two free checked bags. (You know you want to go outlet shopping when you are here!) If you are booking your trip a little later Wow Air might be the good choice. It can be cheaper because it is a no frills airline but you have to be aware of what you are and are not getting.

Getting to Venue

To be honest your life will be easier if you or someone you know has a car. However it would be possible for you to use Super Shuttles and Uber to get around. Once you make it to Williamsport everything is really close.

Car Rentals

Cheap Tickets is a great place to book a car. Lauren swears by it. If you are booking a domestic flight do it through cheap tickets and get the car at the same time. Way easier.

Budget Tip

Megabus, Bolt Bus and Vamoose Bus are great options to get you to the DC area on the cheap. You can also look into MARC trains which go up to Williamsport. But, just ask us or call someone you know and hitch a ride, it's probably easier.
