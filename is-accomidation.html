Hotels

There are not many hotels in Ísafjörður, and in the summer they tend to fill up. If you want to get a hotel room you should do that early.
Hotel Ísafjörður

Airbnb

In Ísafjörður, Airbnb might be your best option. You can find many places in downtown Ísafjörður and in neighboring towns, just don't go too far away unless you plan to have a car. Public transit is limited and there is no Uber.
